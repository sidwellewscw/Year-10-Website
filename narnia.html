The Chronicles of Narnia, by C.S. Lewis

The Chronicles of Narnia by C.S. Lewis is a 7-book fantasy series that centres around four siblings; Peter, Susan, Edmund, and Lucy. While staying in the country, they discover a magical woodrobe that is a doorway to the country Narnia. They travel through the wardrobe and meet lots of interesting chracters including Aslan the lion.The four children have many adventures over the course of the series. Note: The first book - The Magician's Nephew - Is technically the first book of the series, however it is not about Peter, Susuan, Edmund and Lucy. Readers may find it helpful to read The Lion, The Witch, and The Wardrobe first.
