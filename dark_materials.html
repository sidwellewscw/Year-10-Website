His Dark Materials, by Phillip Pullman

His Dark Materials by Phillip Pullman is a three book fantasy science series. It centeres around a young girl Lyra and her daemon, Pantalaimon, who live among scholars at Jordan College. After her friend Roger is kidnapped, she travels to Svalbard and with Gypsies and ends up visiting several parallel worlds. In the second book, the second main character, Will Parry, is introducd. This series is based in a fictional world, however it has many similarities to our own world. The books have enough fantasy to entertain anyone, but also includes elements of science. Note: The first book in the series, The Northern Lights, is also published under the name The Golden Compass.
