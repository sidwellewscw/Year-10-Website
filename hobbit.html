The Hobbit

The Hobbit by J.R.R. Tolkien, is a exciting novel set in the fantasy world of Middle Earth. It centres around the hobbit Biblo Baggins, who goes on an adventure with Gandalf the wizard and 13 dwarves. This is a great book for people who are just getting into the fantasy genre. This book is a prequel to the bestselling series The Lord Of The Rings (make sure you read the Hobbit before the main series, as the Hobbit has lots of useful information and context that will be helpful when reading Lord Of The Rings). Note: The Hobbit is also called There And Back Again.
